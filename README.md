## Overview

**Super Slime** is a casual arcade game where players control a small slime creature that grows by consuming objects in its environment. Starting from a tiny size, the slime explores the map, absorbs smaller items, and gradually becomes larger and more powerful. The core objective is simple: **eat, grow, and explore**. As the slime increases in size, it unlocks the ability to consume bigger objects, creating a satisfying progression loop.

The goal of this project is to recreate similar gameplay and mechanics in form of a short playable advertisement.

---

## Core Features
* Directional movement via touch-based interface.
* Consume objects to gain experience (EXP) and level up, increasing the slime’s size.
* As the slime grows, more areas and larger objects become accessible.
* Built to deliver a complete and engaging experience within a limited time frame.

---

## Gameplay Flow
1. The player starts as a small slime.
2. Explore the environment to locate smaller objects.
3. Consume objects to gain EXP.
4. Level up and increase in size.
5. Unlock the ability to consume larger objects.
6. Continue growing and exploring until the timer ends.
7. A Call-To-Action (CTA) is displayed (e.g., download prompt).
